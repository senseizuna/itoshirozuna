⚔ HOW TO PLAY

1. Build your program by tapping command buttons on the right.

2. Press RUN AI CODE to watch your hero execute your commands.

3. Use STEP to test one command at a time.

4. FORWARD / BACK — move your hero in the direction it's facing.

5. TURN LEFT / RIGHT — rotate your hero without moving.

6. ATTACK — strike the enemy directly ahead of your hero.

7. SHIELD — deflect traps and magic hazards in front of you.

8. COLLECT GEM — pick up a gem on your current tile.

9. ADD LOOP — repeat the entire program N times.

10. Fewer commands = more ⭐ stars. Think like a clever AI!

KEYBOARD: Arrow keys move, A=Attack, S=Shield, C=Collect, Space/Enter=Run, R=Reset
