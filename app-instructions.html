How to use each experience

AI Research Meditation Sanctuary PlasmaMind Racers Pong FracTile Kai Certificate
Research App

Fractal AI Research

How it works: Launch the simulation, start the training flow, and adjust fractal scale, power, and obstacle settings while the AI navigates the environment.

What it does: Visualizes AI behavior in a fractal-driven space with dynamic pathing and interaction loops.

Best use: Great for experimentation, concept demos, and exploring AI + fractal visual storytelling.

Wellness App

Fractal Meditation

How it works: Open the app, choose a meditation mode, and let the visual rhythm and audio pacing guide the session.

What it does: Combines calming fractal motion with meditative atmosphere for relaxation and focus.

Best use: Ideal for mindfulness, breathwork, creative resets, and low-stimulation focus sessions.

Immersive App

Virtual Fractal Sanctuary

How it works: Enter the environment, move through the fractal landscape, and interact with portals or energy points as you explore.

What it does: Creates a contemplative fractal world for visual immersion and exploration.

Best use: Excellent for inspiration, atmosphere, decompression, and imaginative worldbuilding.

Consciousness App

PlasmaMind

How it works: Use the interface to start the experience, follow the visual prompts, and optionally switch into a more immersive mode if supported.

What it does: Delivers a guided fractal atmosphere centered on contemplation and inner focus.

Best use: Useful for reflective sessions, creative decompression, and visual meditation.

Arcade Game

Fractal Racers

How it works: Pick your racer, enter the arena, and use keyboard controls to weave through the neon track while avoiding hazards.

What it does: Brings fast-paced fractal racing into a neon combat-style environment.

Best use: Great for action gameplay, reflex practice, and showing off bold futuristic visuals.

Arcade Game

Fractal Pong

How it works: Choose mouse or keyboard control, rally the ball, and build momentum as the speed increases with each exchange.

What it does: Delivers a clean neon pong experience with combo feedback, score tracking, and fullscreen play.

Best use: Perfect for quick sessions, reflex sharpening, and a lightweight polished arcade experience.

Sandbox Game

FracTile

How it works: Enter a world seed or generate one randomly, move with WASD, mine with left click, place blocks with right click, and explore the minimap as you build.

What it does: Generates a procedural fractal voxel-style sandbox with biomes, shards, mining, building, and repeatable world seeds.

Best use: Great for relaxed exploration, procedural world demos, and creative sandbox sessions.

Kid's Zone Game

Kai Wisdom Fighters

How it works: Pick a monster, solve math problems, and use the answers to power attacks and progress through enemy encounters.

What it does: Turns learning into battle-driven gameplay with progression, visual effects, and educational challenge levels.

Best use: Ideal for kids, parents, and educators who want math practice to feel like an adventure game.

Kid's Zone Reward

Fractal Hunters Certificate

How it works: Enter the child’s details, customize the achievement information, and generate a printable certificate.

What it does: Creates a polished recognition page for milestones, game progress, or learning achievements.

Best use: Great for motivation, classroom rewards, and celebrating progress in a tangible way.
