App Instructions

Note: These apps work best in VR or on a PC browser. Full functionality may be limited on mobile devices due to performance constraints.

Learn how to use the apps, what they do, and how they can enhance your experience.

Fractal AI Research

How It Works: Launch the app in your browser, click "Start AI Training" to begin the simulation, and use the controls to adjust fractal scale, power, and obstacles like chasers and barriers. The AI navigates a 3D fractal environment to collect energy points.

What It Does: This app simulates an AI exploring a 3D fractal space using reinforcement learning, visualizing its path and interactions with dynamic elements like energy points and obstacles.

What It Can Be Used For: Ideal for researchers, students, or enthusiasts interested in AI, fractals, or 3D simulations. Use it to study reinforcement learning, fractal geometry, or to create visually stunning AI-driven art.

Fractal Meditation

How It Works: Open the app in your browser, select a meditation mode (e.g., guided or ambient), and immerse yourself in a dynamic fractal visualization with soothing audio. Adjust settings like color and speed to personalize your experience.

What It Does: Generates an interactive fractal visualization paired with calming sounds to guide users into a meditative state, promoting relaxation and mindfulness.

What It Can Be Used For: Perfect for meditation, stress relief, or enhancing focus. Use it during mindfulness practices, yoga sessions, or as a calming visual aid for relaxation.

Virtual Fractal Sanctuary

How It Works: Access the app in your browser, navigate the 3D fractal environment using mouse or touch controls, and interact with elements like portals and energy nodes to explore different fractal landscapes.

What It Does: Creates an immersive 3D fractal world where users can explore, interact, and customize their surroundings, offering a virtual escape into a metaphysical sanctuary.

What It Can Be Used For: Great for creative inspiration, relaxation, or virtual exploration. Use it to spark imagination, as a creative writing prompt, or as a digital space for mental respite.

PlasmaMind: Journey Through Consciousness

How It Works: Open the app in your browser, use the UI buttons ("Start Meditation," "Toggle Guide," "VR Mode") to interact with the fractal visualization, and follow the guided meditation prompts displayed at the bottom of the screen. Optionally, enter VR mode for a more immersive experience.

What It Does: Provides an immersive 3D fractal environment with a pulsating central sphere, accompanied by guided meditation prompts and calming audio to facilitate consciousness expansion.

What It Can Be Used For: Ideal for meditation, exploring altered states of consciousness, or enhancing spiritual practices. Use it to deepen your mindfulness routine, engage in self-reflection, or as a tool for metaphysical exploration.

Fractal Racers: Tron Edition

How It Works: Launch the app in your browser, select a racer and track, and use keyboard controls (arrow keys or WASD) to navigate the neon-lit fractal tracks. Avoid obstacles and collect power-ups to gain speed boosts.

What It Does: Offers a high-speed racing game set in a 3D fractal environment inspired by Tron, with dynamic tracks, customizable racers, and competitive gameplay.

What It Can Be Used For: Perfect for gaming enthusiasts, casual players, or those interested in fractal-based visuals. Use it for entertainment, to test reflexes, or as a fun way to explore fractal geometry in a gaming context.

Kai Wisdom Fighters

How It Works: Open the app in your browser, follow the tutorial to select a monster, and solve math problems to attack enemies. Use the UI to customize monsters, adjust grade levels (1st to 6th), and toggle music or full-screen mode.

What It Does: Combines math-based gameplay with a monster battling system, featuring 3D visuals, elemental effects, and progressive levels to defeat bosses.

What It Can Be Used For: Ideal for children learning math, educators, or parents seeking engaging educational tools. Use it to practice arithmetic, improve problem-solving skills, or as a fun classroom activity.

Kid's Zone

Kai Wisdom Fighters

How It Works: Access the Kid's Zone via the navigation menu, select Kai Wisdom Fighters, and engage in math-based battles. Choose a monster, solve math problems to attack, and progress through levels to defeat bosses. Customize your monster's appearance and select appropriate grade levels.

What It Does: Provides an educational game where kids solve math problems to power their monster's attacks in a vibrant 3D world, encouraging learning through play.

What It Can Be Used For: Designed for young learners to practice math in an engaging, game-based format. Use it at home or in school to make learning fun and interactive.

Certificate

How It Works: Access the Certificate page via the Kid's Zone menu, enter the child's name and achievement details, and generate a printable certificate. Follow prompts to customize and download the certificate.

What It Does: Creates personalized certificates to reward children for achievements, such as completing levels in Kai Wisdom Fighters or other milestones.

What It Can Be Used For: Perfect for parents, teachers, or educators to motivate and recognize children's accomplishments. Use it to celebrate progress in learning or gaming challenges.
