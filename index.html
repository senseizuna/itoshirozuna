About Itoshiro Zuna

Itoshiro Zuna, meaning ‘one who weaves the thread of eras,’ is a visionary author. Inspired by a childhood memory under a metal arch and a meditation vision with my boxer Akaya, I crafted *The Fractal Symphony of Time* to explore time as a living, fractal force. Dedicated to my wife, children, parents, and Akaya’s memory, this book is my invitation to see yourself as a creator in the cosmic melody.
